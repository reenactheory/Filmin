Filmin Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 21, 2026

1. Information We Collect

Filmin (the "App") does not collect any personal information. The App requires no account registration, and there is no login.

All data you enter into the App (film roll information, camera details, uploaded photos, etc.) is stored only on your device. The App does not transmit any data to external servers and does not perform cloud backups.

3. Third-Party Services and Tracking

The App does not use any advertising networks, analytics tools (such as Google Analytics or Firebase), or third-party SDKs. We do not track your activity in any way.

Write access to your photo library is requested only when you choose to save the "View Entire Film at a Glance" image to your Photos. This permission is used solely at the moment of saving — we do not read your photos or transmit them anywhere.

Uninstalling the App permanently deletes all stored data (rolls, cameras, photos) from your device. No separate server-side deletion request is necessary.
